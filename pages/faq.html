Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers about costs, savings, and equipment.

How much does a typical system cost?
It depends on size, roof, and equipment. Many homes land between 2–10 kW. See our sample packages on the home page and request a custom quote.
What maintenance is required?
Solar PV is low-maintenance. Keep panels clear of heavy debris and snow; we monitor performance and can service issues if they arise.
Will solar work during winter?
Yes—cold temperatures can improve panel efficiency. Production is lower in winter due to sun angle and snow, but summer makes up the difference.
What warranties are included?
Panel performance warranties are typically 25 years; inverter warranties 10–15 years; our workmanship warranty covers installation quality.
