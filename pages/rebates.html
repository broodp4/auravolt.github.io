Programs vary by province, utility, and year. Use the links below to start.

How to claim incentives

  1. Check active provincial/utility programs.
  2. Complete any required energy assessments.
  3. Submit application before purchasing (if required).
  4. Keep all itemized invoices and permits.

Always verify current eligibility and amounts with official sources.
