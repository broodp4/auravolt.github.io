Our Story

Built on Accountability

AuraVolt was founded in 2026 by a team of Red Seal electricians who were frustrated watching homeowners get burned by solar contractors who overpromised, outsourced the real work, and disappeared after installation.

We built AuraVolt differently: every consultation, design, permit application, installation, and maintenance visit is performed by our own certified team. No middlemen. Full accountability.

We're now taking on our first installations across BC — from Vancouver to Kelowna — with the same crew who will back every system we install for decades to come.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Accountability

Every project is owned by our team from start to finish. We never hand your project to a subcontractor. If something needs fixing, we're there — no finger pointing, no delay.

Transparency

We give you honest projections, real numbers, and clear timelines before you sign anything. No bait-and-switch pricing. No surprise fees at installation.

Craftsmanship

Our certified Red Seal electricians and Solar PV installers take pride in every mount, every connection, every conduit run. We build systems that last decades in BC's toughest conditions.

Community

We're a local BC company, not a national franchise. Every dollar you spend on solar stays in BC. We invest in our crews, our community, and BC's clean energy future.
