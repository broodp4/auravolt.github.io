Our Team

K. Dulanja
Renewable Energy Professional
Residential & Commercial Solar PV

K. Dulanja is a highly experienced renewable energy professional with over a decade of hands-on experience in the solar power industry. He holds an Advanced Diploma in Engineering Technology from the University of Moratuwa, SL (2012) and an MBA from Cardiff Metropolitan University, UK, 2021 combining strong technical expertise with strategic and business-focused leadership. He began his career in renewable energy in 2013 as an Assistant Engineer and has since played a key role in the planning, design, and implementation of over 500 renewable energy projects, contributing to a total installed capacity of more than 50 MWp. His international experience spans Sri Lanka, India, Kenya, and Canada, giving him a strong understanding of diverse energy markets and project environments. One of his notable achievements includes serving as the Project Engineer for the Eluvathiv Mini-Grid Project, the first hybrid Solar–Wind–Diesel mini-grid system on the east coast of Sri Lanka. This landmark project provided reliable electricity to 180 rural households, significantly improving quality of life and supporting local development. Since relocating to British Columbia, Canada, K. Dulanja has successfully delivered over 100 residential and commercial Solar PV projects, offering expertise across the full project life cycle—from site assessment and system design to installation, commissioning, and long-term maintenance. He is deeply passionate about advancing clean energy solutions and is committed to delivering high-quality workmanship, reliable after-sales service, and customer-focused solutions. With a strong vision for a sustainable future, he is enthusiastic about expanding access to green energy across British Columbia
