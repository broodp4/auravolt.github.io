The Process

How Our Solar Installation Works

Your road to renewable energy — guided by our dedicated in-house team every step of the way.

01

Consultation & Design

Custom design built around your roof and your savings
  • Detailed site assessment using satellite imaging and roof measurements
  • 3D modelling for new builds to determine optimal system size
  • Tailored design to maximize bill reduction, with battery storage options
  • Clear projection of cost, production, and long-term savings upfront
02

Permitting & Rebates

We tackle BC's toughest paperwork
  • End-to-end permit management: city approvals, electrical permits, BC Hydro forms
  • We apply for all available BC Hydro rebates and incentives on your behalf
  • In-house handling means fewer delays vs. contractors who outsource
  • Faster approval, full accountability — nothing missed
03

Professional Installation

Certified installers. Built to weather BC.
  • Red Seal electricians and certified Solar PV installers — no subcontractors
  • Every panel and connection meets Technical Safety BC standards
  • Engineered to withstand snow, rain, and coastal weather province-wide
  • Full accountability and consistent craftsmanship guaranteed
04

Activation & Support

Your system stays our responsibility
  • Full system activation and testing to confirm production targets
  • Ongoing monitoring, maintenance, and annual inspections included
  • Future expansions — battery, EV charging, off-grid — same trusted crew
  • Local support from the same team who installed your system
