Frequently asked questions

How do you price projects?
We use OpenSolar to design systems and produce detailed, itemized proposals. You’ll see hardware, labour, and any incentives separated clearly.

No. We provide an initial OpenSolar design and savings estimate with no commitment.
What warranties are included?
Panels typically carry 25-year output warranties; inverters 10–15 years; and AuraVolt provides a workmanship warranty on installation.
